Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how RubenB Games ("we", "us", or "our") handles information in connection with our games and this website (rubenbgames.com).

1. Information We Collect

Our games may collect the following information:

  • Epic Account information — When you log in via Epic Games, we receive basic account data (account ID, display name) provided by Epic Online Services for the purpose of enabling multiplayer sessions. We do not store this data on our own servers.
  • Local game settings — Settings such as keybinds, sensitivity, and preferences are stored locally on your device and are not transmitted to us.
  • Session data — Temporary session information (such as lobby details) is handled by Epic Online Services infrastructure and is not stored by us beyond the duration of a session.

2. How We Use Information

Information is used solely to:

  • Enable online multiplayer functionality (session creation, discovery, and joining)
  • Authenticate players via Epic Online Services
  • Save local game preferences on your device

3. Third-Party Services

Our games use Epic Online Services (EOS) for multiplayer functionality. Epic Games' own privacy policy governs the data they collect. You can review it at epicgames.com/privacypolicy.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your information to third parties. Data passed through Epic Online Services is governed by Epic Games' privacy policy.

5. Data Retention

We do not retain personal data on our own servers. Any data processed through Epic Online Services is subject to Epic's data retention policies.

6. Children's Privacy

Our games are not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

8. Contact
