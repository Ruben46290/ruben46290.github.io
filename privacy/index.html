Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how RubenB Games ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, shares, and retains information in connection with our games and this website (rubenbgames.com). By using our games or website, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Capture (How Data Is Captured)

We collect only the minimum data necessary to provide our services. Data is captured through the following mechanisms:

a) Epic Account Services (EAS) Login

When you choose to log in via Epic Games to access online multiplayer features, Epic Online Services (EOS) transmits the following account data to our game client at the time of authentication:

  • Epic Account ID — a unique identifier assigned by Epic Games
  • Display name — your Epic Games display name

This data is received by the game client on your device for the sole purpose of establishing a multiplayer session. It is captured automatically as part of the Epic Online Services SDK authentication flow when you initiate login.

b) Session Data

When you host or join an online multiplayer session, session-related data (such as lobby identifiers, player slot information, and connection metadata) is temporarily processed by Epic Online Services infrastructure. This data is generated automatically when a session is created or joined and exists only for the duration of that session.

c) Local Device Data

Our games store the following data locally on your device, captured when you modify settings in-game:

  • Keybind preferences
  • Mouse sensitivity and invert settings
  • Audio and display preferences
  • Game progress and save data

This data is written to local save files on your device and is never transmitted to us or any third party.

d) Website

Our website (rubenbgames.com) does not use cookies, tracking pixels, analytics scripts, or any form of user tracking. No personal data is collected through the website.

2. How Data Is Used

Data captured through the above mechanisms is used exclusively for the following purposes:

Data Type Purpose
Epic Account ID & Display Name To authenticate you with Epic Online Services and associate you with a multiplayer session. Not used for any marketing, profiling, or analytics purposes.
Session Data To create, discover, and join multiplayer lobbies. Used only during an active session and discarded when the session ends.
Local Settings & Save Data To restore your preferences and game progress on your own device. Never transmitted or used outside your local machine.

We do not use any collected data for advertising, behavioural profiling, analytics, or any purpose beyond what is stated above.

3. How Data Is Shared

We do not sell, rent, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal data to any third parties for commercial purposes.

Data sharing occurs only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Epic Online Services — Account ID and session data is shared with Epic Games' servers as a technical requirement of using their multiplayer infrastructure. This sharing is governed by Epic Games' Privacy Policy.
  • Other players in a session — Your display name may be visible to other players within the same multiplayer session for identification purposes.
  • Legal requirements — We may disclose data if required to do so by law or in response to valid legal process.

No data is shared with advertisers, data brokers, analytics providers, or any other third parties beyond those listed above.

4. How Data Is Retained

We do not operate our own servers or databases, and therefore we do not retain any personal data on our end.

Data Type Where Stored Retention Period
Epic Account ID & Display Name Processed in memory on your device during gameplay; handled by Epic Online Services servers Not stored by us. Retention by Epic Games is governed by their privacy policy.
Session Data (lobby/connection info) Epic Online Services servers Automatically deleted when the session ends. Not stored by us.
Local Settings & Save Data Your local device only Retained until you uninstall the game or manually delete save files. Never transmitted to us.

5. Third-Party Services

Our games use Epic Online Services (EOS), provided by Epic Games, Inc., for online multiplayer functionality including authentication, session management, and peer-to-peer networking. Epic Games operates independently and their collection and use of data is governed by their own privacy policy, available at epicgames.com/privacypolicy.

6. Your Rights

Since we do not store personal data on our own servers, there is no personal data held by us to access, correct, or delete. For data held by Epic Games through their services, please refer to Epic Games' privacy policy and their data subject request process.

7. Children's Privacy

Our games are not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided personal information through our services, please contact us and we will take appropriate steps.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of our games or website after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
