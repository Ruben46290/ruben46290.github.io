Trailer / Gameplay Video
Description

Open chests to get cards that boost your chests. Buy special powerups to boost progression. Relatively simple clicker game where the core gameplay is pressing 1-9 on your keypad to match the highlighted number on the grid. Wait for your chests to open to get cards. Upgrade your cards with gold to get a stat bonus.

More Information

This game was made in around 2 weeks as an exercise in making a polished clicker game. Balanced to only last around 10 minutes due to the lack of variety in gameplay. This was the first project where I had a full saving and loading system and I really got to learn the ins and outs of using one.
